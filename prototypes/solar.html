The system.

A game where your real body is the character. Six stats, infinite levels, streaks that earn real rewards.

01 — Stats

Six attributes. One body.

Every workout maps to Strength, Endurance, Flexibility, Velocity, Equilibrium, Vitality. Read your character sheet like a player.

02 — Levels

Infinite tiers. Real progression.

Climb from Novice to Legend. Reset with prestige and start over with a badge that says you've proved it once.

03 — Streaks

Weeks, not days.

The unit of consistency is a week. Each week you both hit target, the streak grows. Each week you don't, it holds.

04 — AI plans

Meals that learn your taste.

Personalized nutrition, monthly with weekly tweaks. Tell it you hated quinoa once. It won't ask again.

05 — Body analysis

Private by architecture.

Optional photo. AI Vision reads composition. Image deleted in 60 seconds — only the numbers stay, only you see them.

06 — Rewards

Real things. Earned by streak.

Your wishlist + partner discounts + AI-suggested gear. Unlocked by the streak you hold, not the level you sit on.
